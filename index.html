Bachelors Thesis

BRDF Capturing in a Light Stage

My bachelor's thesis focused on the topic of "BRDF Capturing in a Light Stage". The goal was to record a BRDF from a material sample which was placed into the middle of the light stage. The recording was done with a camera, which pipeline I edited and suited for my needs. This pipeline utilized C++ to record images based on different light setting, capturing the material sample and calculating the BRDF data. Additionally, Python was used for data analysis and to visualize data. The experiment was conducted in the experimental laboratory of the University of Tübingen, employing both remote and on-site control.
